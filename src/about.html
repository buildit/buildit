About People Find us

We are

Battle-tested

Resilience is our most valuable quality, with teams that have ownership, commitment and the ability to adapt to unique organisational aspirations and challenges

Innovative

Self-confessed geeks, always curious to explore technology and better ways of working that deliver maximum business impact

No ‘bull’

From open-minded to open source, as natural collaborators we are not afraid to ‘do the right thing’ for our clients and their customers

Problem solvers

Managing complexity is our raison d’etre, adapting to users, building within frameworks, and maintaining the big picture

We’re looking for …

Colleagues who want to help us change the way companies think and approach problems;

Colleagues who want to work with teams and individuals who share a passion for solving complex problems;

Colleagues who want to own the problem, whether it’s about technology, ways of working, teams, or customer experience.

Individuals who want to be treated like adults;

Individuals who neither need, nor want, someone else to tell them what needs to be done, but instead can look at what’s happening, what’s needed, and figure out how they fit into it;

Individuals who are comfortable finding their own place.

People who want to work in cross-functional teams, and want to help others learn how to build them, and why they're valuable;

People who understand when a technology is right, and when it isn't;

People with a grasp of a variety of technologies, languages, and methodologies, along with their pros and cons.
