Our values

Self Managed

We don’t discuss unimportant things at length

We avoid bureaucracy wherever possible

We take pride in eliminating things that aren’t valuable and automate things that are

If it’s a simple problem, we just fix it

We work on what’s important, not just interesting or easy

We don’t build a full product if all we need is an MVP; we launch early and iterate

We get many views when the problem is complex

Encourage growth

We actively seek feedback

We strive for keen awareness of our own limitations

We hold and express opinions, and modify them when faced with contrary evidence

Trust

When things don’t work out, we have honest post-mortems

We believe in our teammates’ abilities

Driven

We do what it takes to achieve a successful outcome

We never stop learning

We try out new things

We expose ourselves to things outside our comfort zone

We ask ‘why’

We take ownership of our own growth

Effective

We are fiercely pragmatic

We find ways to make complicated tasks easy, not easy things complicated.

Supportive

We lead from the front, know when to step back

We make time to help when asked

We share what we know

We exert influence through what we know and do, not titles or hierarchy

Reliable, Dependable

Wherever possible, we let the facts and metrics do the talking

Mastery of what we do comes, first and foremost, from real-world practice

We weigh up cost and benefit of improvements

We monitor and make visible the effect of our changes

We don’t commit to something we know we don’t have the resources to do

We dare to fail fast; we know when to cut our losses

Genuinely curious

We actively seek to understand other functions and their value

We are open-minded: when people disagree, we listen and digest

Proactive

We are doers

We pull our own weight

We bring up problems as soon as we notice them

We take ownership; we don’t wait to be told what to do

Before asking for permission, we ask ourselves whether we need permission (or just seek forgiveness)

We take responsibility for carving out our own contribution to the overall proposition

Humility

We bring an ego-less mindset to the group

We never look up, never look down

Expertise

Whatever we are doing, we can describe why

We make our work visible

We explain the rationale behind decisions

Integrity

We take responsibility for our decisions

We are honest and open about issues, delays, or problems

When we don’t know, or can’t, we say so
